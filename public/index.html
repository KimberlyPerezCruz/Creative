Hello!

My name is Kimberly Perez. I created this website to express my opinion on what I think feminism really is and how much I have learned about it in the class WMST-100 at George Mason University. I also wanted to provide the opportunity to others to express their opinion.

After clicking on the Start button, you will be able to see a slide show of gif containing a message about why we need feminism. The last photo of the slide contains a link that would provide you the opportunity of choosing an image and write a message to add to the slide show.

When you are ready, click on start.
